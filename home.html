About Us

Flippper is a modern twist on the classic book — a multi-display, ring-bound smartbook made of flexible ePaper screens. It aims to combine the best of both worlds: the tangible, focused experience of reading physical books with the power, flexibility, and sustainability of modern digital tools. Students can download textbooks, write with real pen input, draw diagrams, or solve problems — all in a paper-like interface that works offline. No glare, no distractions, no paper waste. We’re exploring if Flippper could replace school books, reduce backpack weight, support focused study, and help the planet. Your feedback will shape the future of education.
